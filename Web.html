Neuro Calm
Recommended Use

Neuro Calm is a practical, evidence-based calming tool for all ages that works to calm the nervous system within minutes, or even seconds.

Use Neuro Calm to help

  • Reduce intense emotions such as anxiety, anger, or fear
  • Improve focus and decrease impulsivity
  • Fall asleep faster and stay asleep

For the best results, use Neuro Calm at the first sign of distress or overwhelm. For parents, using Neuro Calm at the earliest signs of a child’s dysregulation can often prevent a meltdown and may even help stop one once it has begun.

Neuro Calm can be used as often as needed, or proactively in the morning and evening, to support the brain’s calming networks and help regulate the nervous system.

Why it Works

When a person's brain and nervous system feel disorganized, the rhythm of a metronome restores a sense of organization. The consistent beat provides an anchor for attention, reduces cognitive load, and synchronizes neural networks across the brain, promoting a calmer, more regulated state.

Directing the sound specifically to the left ear stimulates the right hemisphere of the brain, which serves to regulate and calm a person’s stress response.

Neuro Calm
Directions

  1. Adjust the metronome to your preferred BPM. 54 BPM is the default and is often most effective for calming.
  2. If desired, turn the Flash toggle on and select a color. *Note: For most people, the flash enhances the calming effect. However, a small number of individuals may experience a startle response. Test the flash first to see how you (or your child) respond before using it with the metronome.
  3. While you (or your child) face forward, hold the phone on the left side of the head so that the sound is directed into the left ear. *Note: Do not hold the phone on the right side of the head. Doing so stimulates excitatory networks in the brain and will have a stimulating effect.
  4. If the flash is on, position the phone so the light flashes primarily into the left eye.
  5. Continue for 1-2 minutes in a dimly lit or dark room.
